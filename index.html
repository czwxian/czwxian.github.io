Zhiwen Chen (陈志文)

I'm currently working as a Staff Algorithm Engineer at Alibaba Group where I'm leading the PixelAI-Body Algorithm Team of Tao Technology. Previously before 2017, I worked as a video analytic researcher in Trakomatic Pte. Ltd., Singapore for several years.

I received the B.E. degree in computer science from SJTU, under the supervision of Prof. Fan Wu in 2012 and the M.E. degree in computer science from NUS in 2014.

Research

I'm interested in computer vision, in particular, 2D&3D Human Body Pose Estimation & Construction. Below are some highlighted publications.

[New] Multiple Pose Virtual Try-On Based on 3D Clothing Reconstruction
Thai Thanh Tuan, Zhiwen Chen, Heejune Ahn, John Wainwright
IEEE Access , 2021  
paper / bibtex

CloTH-VTON+: Clothing Three-dimensional reconstruction for Hybrid image-based Virtual Try-ON
Zhiwen Chen, Thai Thanh Tuan, Heejune Ahn
IEEE Access , 2021  
project page / paper / bibtex

We proposed a novel hybrid and fully automatic method for 3D clothing reconstruction from single image and applying it to image-based virtual try-on (VTON) for fashion clothing, which generates realistically deformed try-on results with superior quality.
